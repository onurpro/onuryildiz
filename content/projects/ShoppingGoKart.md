**Role:** Team Lead

## Project Overview

I led a team of friends over the summer of 2024 in the design, construction, and testing of a fully functional electric go-kart capable of up to **25 km/h**, built from a repurposed shopping cart chassis. We held weekly meetings to plan and execute the project, designing and integrating all systems from scratch.

## What It Does

The electric shopping go-kart is a fully functional, electrified vehicle that moves under its own power using a **1000 W motor** integrated with the rear axle. It features a custom **740 Wh battery pack** built from Samsung 30Q 18650 cells that I soldered together by hand.

## Development Process

1. Brainstormed project ideas and decided on repurposing a shopping cart.
2. Researched electric drivetrains and power delivery systems.
3. Designed the powertrain and integrated it into the shopping cart frame.
4. Held weekly team meetings to refine the design and address challenges.
5. Assembled and tested the completed electric shopping go-kart.

### My Contributions

- **Team Leadership:** Led the overall project direction, coordinating weekly meetings and task delegation.
- **Powertrain Design:** Designed the full powertrain — motor, battery, and power delivery system.
- **Battery Pack Assembly:** Soldered Samsung 30Q 18650 cells into a 740 Wh pack.
- **Motor Integration:** Integrated the 1000 W motor with the rear axle to simplify manufacturing.
- **Testing:** Collaborated with the team on testing, troubleshooting, and iterating on the design.

## Key Learnings

I gained hands-on experience with electric powertrain design, battery pack assembly, and motor integration. Leading the team taught me how to coordinate across different workstreams and make engineering trade-offs under real constraints.
